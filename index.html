Massage

Massage therapy allows our clients to relax an revive. Using manual movements and manipulation of the soft tissues and muscles in the body promotes healing and enhances a person’s well-being. There are many forms of massage therapy, each with their own benefits. No longer is massage thought of as a luxury: Treatment is available for anyone, whether for relaxation purposes, for sports recovery or for pain relief. ​

Rehabilitation

Anyone who leads an active lifestyle, participates in sport or even just enjoys a Sunday walk can incur some kind of injury. Injuries can be caused by a traumatic incident during a game or competition, or through gradual onset due to dysfunctional movement patterns, getting worse as time goes by.

Neurokinetic Therapy

Neurokinetic Therapy (NKT) is suitable for patients with various musculoskeletal & neurological conditions. Based on motor control theory NKT involves a thorough examination of the entire body using a system of precise tests to identify muscle imbalances and dysfunctions. Specific exercises are then provided to address any imbalances, which will allow optimisation of bodily movements and return of prior function.

Accupuncture

Acupuncture is an ancient Chinese medicine-based approach to treating a variety of conditions by triggering specific points on the skin with needle. Acupuncture is a minimally invasive method to stimulate nerve-rich areas of the skin surface in order to influence tissues, gland, organs, and various functions of the body.
